What is the job outlook like?

High demand is expected for information security analysts. Cyberattacks have grown in frequency, and these analysts will be needed to create innovative solutions to prevent hackers from stealing critical information or creating problems for computer networks. The growth rate for information security analysts are quite high, as job opportunities rise 17.9% every year within the United States. Projected to grow up to 32 percent in 2032.

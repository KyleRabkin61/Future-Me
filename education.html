What are the education requirements?

Many Information Security Analysts have bachelor's degrees in a field related to computer science, however, other disciplines that rely on critical and analytical thinking are also relevant. Master's level work in information assurance/information security is increasingly common, as are graduate-level academic certificates. Some qualities required for this job include analytical, communication, problem-solving, and creative skills, as well as being detail oriented.
